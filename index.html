How does the virtual lipstick try-on work?
Upload a photo or take a selfie, choose a lipstick shade, and our AI technology applies the color to your lips realistically.
Are my photos stored?
No. Your photos are processed temporarily for the AI effect and are not permanently stored on our servers.
Do I need an account to use the app?
Yes, you need to sign up and sign in to upload photos or take selfies. Registration is free.
What is the generation system?
Some features use a generation-based system. Each image processing consumes 1 generation. Generations can be purchased in-app.
Can I get a refund for purchased generations?
Purchased digital generations are non-refundable. If generations cannot be used due to a technical error, please contact our support team.
How can I delete my account?
You can delete your account from the "Delete Account" section in the menu.

How Does the Generation System Work?

Generations are used to try lipstick colors in this app.

  • Each lipstick try-on uses 1 generation
  • You can try different colors on a single photo
  • Generation packages are for monthly use
  • Unused generations do not carry over to the next month

You can track your generation balance from your profile page.

Important Information

  • Each lipstick application consumes 1 generation
  • Generations are automatically refunded for failed operations
